# N-Best 2008 (Broadcast News NL)

The N-Best 2008 evaluation campaign corpus for Dutch broadcast news ASR. Contains BN-NL (broadcast news, Netherlands accent) and BN-VL (Flemish accent) recordings with NIST focus conditions F0 (clean), F1 (spontaneous), F2 (telephone/interview). Produced by TNO, Radboud University, and the Dutch Language Institute (INL).
